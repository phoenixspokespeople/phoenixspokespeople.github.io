Every Friday morning we'd love for you to join us at:
  • Bike Friendly Friday Ride to Work, which meets at Rollover Doughnuts at 7 a.m.
    Enjoy some coffee and treats before heading downtown at about 7:30. The route is 2.4 miles along the [5th Avenue Sonoran Bikeway, ending at City Hall](https://goo.gl/maps/KFeDWRWET392).
    There's a Grid Bike Share station out in front and the light rail stop is Central and Encanto.

There's plenty more bike fun going on in Phoenix.
Here are other events Phoenix Spokes People recommend:

Every Monday evening you can join:
  • Phoenix Downtempo Ride, a shorter, relaxed, and social ride which stop at a bar, and usually ends at Motown on Mondays.
  • Loops PHX, hosted by State Bicycle Co. All bikes welcome, fixed encouraged. They make 1-3 stops and ride 10-20 miles.
Every Thursday evening you can join:
  • WTF Phoenix Ride, a ride for any women, trans*, femme, and gender non-conforming folks! No CIS guys.
The Last Friday of the month is:
  • Critical Mass Phoenix, a bicycle celebration ride to raise awareness about bicycles being on the road and cars sharing the road.
